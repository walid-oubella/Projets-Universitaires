Charter of the IUT of Villetaneuse


Preamble :


We remind you that the Students' Office (BDE) is first and foremost an association for the benefit of students in order to allow them an extracurricular development. It is illustrated by a room at the disposal of the students where the values of the Republic must reign: freedom, equality and fraternity. But also the fundamental principles of the Constitution such as the respect of others. For this, everyone must know the common rules cited in the following charter.


1. Respect of people:


  • 1.1 To have a respectful behavior towards the different people present in the BDE
  • 1.2 Do not engage in violent or insulting behavior towards a student
  • 1.3 Do not make racist, homophobic or sexist comments to a student
  • 1.4 Respect the principle of gender equality

2. Respect of property :


  • 2.1 To respect the various movable goods of the BDE: sofas, microwaves, TV, table, chairs and piano
  • 2.2 Do not damage the furniture or the various games provided for the students
  • 2.3 Request permission to open windows
  • 2.4 Request authorization for the use of the TV
  • 2.5 Respect the piano's schedule
  • 2.6 Putting away chairs after use
  • 2.7 Respect the cleanliness and tidiness of the room (e.g., throw away trash)

3. Respect for the school environment :


  • 3.1 Respect the noise level outside of lunch breaks
  • 3.2 Do not create a crowd in front of the BDE room
  • 3.3 Respect any teacher who enters the room

⚠ Failure to comply with these rules may result in exclusion from the premises.
